## Professional Background

I work at the intersection of technology, community, and innovation. I thrive on empowering developers to create and innovate. I believe that interactive software is a powerful art form. I am a software engineer, developer advocate, and an accomplished public speaker. I have run a game studio, written production code for many major enterprises, and presented to thousands of students and developers at many major industry events and countless tech meetups across the world.

### Career History

After graduating from college, I co-founded Yobonja. An indie game studio best known for the game _Blast Monkeys_, which was the #1 Android app for over six months in 2011.

In 2013, I joined Microsoft as a Technical Evangelist. There I helped developers leverage Microsoft platforms and technology. I solved developer's technical challenges, built prototypes, and trained developers in industry-leading engineering best practices. Eventually, I became a Senior Software Engineer within the Industrial Solutions Engineering team. I helped Microsoft invest in top customers and partners in a variety of industries, solving their critical business needs. I went from designing architecture all the way through production writing code alongside the customer's engineering teams.

I also ran a solo venture, PlayPerro, from 2012 to 2024. There I experimented with interactive experiences. My game _#DanceTogether_ was featured at IndieCade and GDC's Experimental Gameplay Workshop.

Since 2024 I have had the privilege of working at Arm as Staff Developer Evangelist.
